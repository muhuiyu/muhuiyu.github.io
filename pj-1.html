UI/UX DESIGN & DEVELOP

Backstage for BotBonnie Admin

Overview
BotBonnie Platform is built for users to create chatbots. With this platform, users can establish their chatbot agent for both Facebook and LINE, the top 2 most popular messaging platforms in Asia.
The front-stage of BotBonnie is a chatbot-building platform. However, since there’re many different platform subscription plans and pricing of BotBonnie Inc, it’s very complicate and inconvenient for company administrators to change their customers’ subscription plans since there’s no any GUI tools can be used.

The Solution
To improve the situation, I designed a new backstage of BotBonnie Inc. for company administrators to manage all customers and their chatbots.

Company
BotBonnie Inc.

THE CHALLENGE

Since there's no other engineers can help me to build the platform, it should be simple, useful, and have better time productivity.

My Role

It is a one-person project, and I worked as UX/UI Designer and full-stack engineer. Some of my responsibilities included:

  • Planning and defining scpoe and schedule
  • Conduct user interviews & observations
  • Facilitate ideation & design decisions
  • Sketching, wireframing, and prototyping
  • Front-end & back-end development
  • User testing and evaluation sessions

The Impact

Currently used by administrators of BotBonnie to manage 982 customers with 1653 products, and analyze the statistics and perfomance of server and platform.

Success Matrics

  • Efficient & save time in chatbot management
  • Should have different authorities for admins
  • Should be easy to analyze and compare statistics

Production

First, administrator logs in to his/her accounts. Then he/she can view all chatbots in the index page. Looking for the target chatbot, and manage the basic information of this chatbot.

TARGET AUDIENCE

Our Users

The users of this platform are the admin assistants, project managers, and marketing assistants of BotBonnie, Inc. They act as an administator to manage customers and update setting of customers' chatbots. Also, they are internal analysts with the goal of driving improvement in quality and finding appropriate strategies.

USER RESERACH

Interviews & Observation

After interviewing with stakeholders and target users, I concluded their needs with information about their behaviors as the following:

  • Most users are not familiar with programming or coding
  • Marketing assistants need all ranking and statistics to make weekly report
  • Admin assistants update or check customers' chatbot more than 30 times per day
  • Admin assistants need to access to customers' chatbot editing platform file
