About Miulab

Miulab (Machine Intelligence and Understanding Laboratory) is a lab of National Taiwan University, which mainly focus on Research of Natural Language Processing, Deep Learning, and Machine Learning.

I was in this lab for 2 years during my college. Since it is a new lab in the college (establish only 2 years), we still have no official lab website. So I was requested to design one. And because all of these research topics of lab are novel and popular nowadays, the lab website should also be designed in a creative and novel way. The analysis of needs and details are as the follows.

User Needs

Requirement

- Easy to find members research interests and experience → clear member list
- Quick and easy to find previous research and demo links → less animations or decoration
- Display the atmosphere of our lab with lively and creative → modern design
- Let people want to corporate with our lab → regularly web updating and lots of demo system
- Sense of science and technology & Modern Design
- With simple user interactions

Structure

- lab info
- lab news
- lab member
- lab previous research
- lab all demo list
- workshops or activities held by labs
- contact info
- search bar

Method

- Wireframe: hand-drawing, illustrator, keynote, photoshop
- Mockup: Sketch
- Prototype: Figma, Sketch, invision
