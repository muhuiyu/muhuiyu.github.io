Group Buying Feature Design

Blay App is a Flight Assistant and friend-making app for users to meet travelers all over the world. It is the world's first app to let you see who is flying to the same destination on the same day as you right after putting in your flight information.

Recently, Blay is making proactive development to create novel social situations. As a consequence, they focused on the social potential of “group buying”, and built up this function as a new feature to make travelers get more social interactions.

In this project, I took references from 拼多多 (Group-Buying More) App, Shopee App, Gomaji App, and Deliveroo App. After collecting and assessing the user activities of Blay App, I completed a whole user flow, interactions, and user interfaces of Group Buying feature in Blay App.

My Role

As the UX and UI designer, I started from product specification definition, user flow, wireframe flow, high-fidelity mockups, and prototyping. Also, I did some usability testing by questionnaires and interviewing to our team members and real users. And finally, I cooperated with engineers to revise my design and complete this task.

The Assignment Briefing

After interviewing with all members in BotBonnie Inc, I summarizes all requirements as the following:

  • The most viewed page is the main page (list of all flight tickets)
  • The feature and user flow design should make users join/start a group buying event.
  • Users can manage and use their coupons after they finish group buying events.

Usability Test

We took usability tests twice. One is for wireframe prototyping, and the other is for our high-fidelity prototyping.

First Testing

For the first time, we only ran the usability test on our team members (including UI designer, business developer, product manager, and back-end engineer) and potential users. Our feedbacks is as follows:

  • Great and smooth user flow to choosing / joining a new group buying event.
  • The notification bar is annoying while using this app.
  • Need more motivating things or events to make users click/view the group buying pages.
