Backstage for BotBonnie Admin

BotBonnie Platform is built for users to create chatbots. With this platform, users can establish their chatbot agent for both Facebook and LINE, the top 2 most popular messaging platforms in Asia.

The front-stage of BotBonnie is a chatbot-building platform. However, since there’re many different platform subscription plans and pricing of BotBonnie Inc, it’s very complicate and inconvenient for company administrators to change their customers’ subscription plans since there’s no any GUI tools can be used.

To improve the situation, I designed a new backstage of BotBonnie Inc. for company administrators to manage all customers and their chatbots.

My Role

It is a one-person project. In this project, I interviewed all company members, constructed user flow & Information Architecture, created wireframes and mockups, and finally, built the front-end interface and back-end server to finish this task. I spent about 3 months (18 workdays) to completely finish this project.

Production

First, administrator logs in to his/her accounts. Then he/she can view all chatbots in the index page. Looking for the target chatbot, and manage the basic information of this chatbot.

The Assignment Briefing

After interviewing with all members in BotBonnie Inc, I summarizes all requirements as the following:

  • A backstage platform for BotBonnie team members and administrators.
  • Used for chatbot management, such as upgrade/degrade and change subscription plans.
  • Admins must have their own login accounts with different authorities.
  • Display the popularity ranking of all chatbots and others Statistics.
  • Since there's no other engineers can help me to build the platform, it should be simple, useful, and have better time productivity.

For the first time, we only ran the usability test on our team members (including UI designer, business developer, product manager, and back-end engineer) and potential users.
